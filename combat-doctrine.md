# Kombat Analyst Combat Doctrine

# Pressure Wrestler

## Core Objective
Close distance, force grappling exchanges,
create cardio pressure, and control positioning.

## Strengths
- Cage pressure
- Takedown chains
- Pace overwhelm
- Neutralizes range striking
- Drains opponent cardio

## Weaknesses
- Submission threats
- Elite scrambling
- Counter knees and intercept strikes
- Fast lateral movement

## Best Against
- Distance Strikers
- Low-output counter fighters
- Pure kickboxers

## Struggles Against
- Submission Hunters
- Elite anti-wrestlers
- Explosive counter grapplers

## Trait Synergy
### Strong Traits
- Pressure Heavy
- Durable
- High Pace

### Weak Traits
- Low Output
- Passive

# Well Rounded MMA Fighter

## Core Objective
Adapt dynamically across striking,
wrestling, grappling, and positional exchanges.

## Strengths
- Adaptability
- Strategic flexibility
- Layered offense
- Matchup versatility

## Weaknesses
- Can lack specialization
- Sometimes less dangerous in key domains
- Vulnerable to elite specialists

## Best Against
- Predictable fighters
- One-dimensional archetypes
- Fighters reliant on single systems

## Struggles Against
- Elite specialists
- Extreme pressure archetypes
- Dominant physical advantages

## Trait Synergy

### Strong Traits
- Technical
- Adaptive
- Patient

### Weak Traits
- Chaos Fighter
- Reckless
